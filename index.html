At Verizon, we collect events from the lives of millions of used cars from 20 European countries, as well as the USA and Canada. We can then create a vehicle history for every car in our database and make it available to you.

The information helps you to check sales data, avoid expensive follow-up costs and negotiate a fair purchase price.
